FirmCast

Co-Founder of FirmCast, a smart scheduling platform for the accounting industry

  • FirmCast is a platform that enables smart scheduling of projects, services and other activities carried out at accounting firms
  • Accounting firms face a problem of scheduling their projects and often, the highest priority, highest paying projects aren't prioritized leading to lower revenues
  • Accountants are also not fully utilized for their time, leading to downtimes at some points in time and overtime at others
  • FirmCast enables smart scheduling based on sophisticated scheduling algorithms and methods that have been proven out in the manufacturing and transportation industry. These methods enable optimized scheduling based on appropriate resource constraints and lead to large efficiency gains in both revenues and accountant productivity
  • Secured initial funding and deals to carry out pilot processes to build and deploy the product at three prospective client firms
