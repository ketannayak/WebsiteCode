Jun 2010 - Aug 2010

Summer Intern

During this internship, I explored a new class of techniques used for wave scattering based imaging. In particular, I investigated a class of methods that could be used to attain super-resolution (resolution beyond Rayleigh limit) by modeling higher order scattering effects. We showed that point scatterers placed closer than the Rayleigh limit can be resolved through this method and that super-resolution could be used to improve resolution of larger objects in elastic media.
